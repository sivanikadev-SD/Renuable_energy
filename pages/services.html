FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Most residential systems are installed in 1–2 days. Commercial systems may take 1–4 weeks depending on size. Grid connection by your utility can add 2–6 weeks.

Most residential solar installations are permitted by right and don't require planning permission. We handle all permits and utility applications on your behalf.

Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days — typically 10–25% of peak output. With a battery storage system, you can store energy for use during low-production periods.

We offer $0-down solar loans at 0% APR for qualified buyers, solar leases, and Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs). We also help you claim federal and state tax credits.

Grid-tied systems without batteries will shut off during an outage for safety reasons. Systems with battery storage can provide backup power during outages.
