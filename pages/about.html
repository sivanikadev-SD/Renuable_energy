Our Mission

Accelerating the
Clean Energy Transition

Founded in 2000, SolarVolt has grown from a small family business installing rooftop solar panels to one of North America's most trusted renewable energy companies, with over 15,000 completed installations across 38 states.

Our mission is simple: make clean, affordable renewable energy accessible to every homeowner and business, while delivering exceptional customer service and industry-leading warranties.
